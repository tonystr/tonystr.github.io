Juju Adams is a London-based 2D programmer and designer, working primarily in GameMaker Studio.

He enjoys building games that push the engine into new territory, technically and narratively, specialising in optimisation and console development.

Outside of work, he dedicates his time to passing on skills to upcoming creators. Juju runs the monthly Unofficial GameMaker Meetup, and a large Discord server for developers of all aptitudes.
